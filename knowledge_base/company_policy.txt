ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

Refund Policy:
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase if they are not satisfied with the service. Refund requests after 14 days require manager approval and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor before processing.

Subscription Cancellation:
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through their account settings. Cancellation requests for annual plans must be approved by a supervisor if requested within the first 30 days of a renewal.

Account Closure:
Account closure requests are permanent and require supervisor approval. Once approved, all customer data is retained for 90 days before permanent deletion, in compliance with data protection regulations.

Compensation Requests:
Any compensation requests due to service outages or errors must be escalated to management for review and approval.

Escalation Policy:
Requests that cannot be resolved by standard support agents, or that involve dissatisfaction with company decisions, must be escalated to management.
