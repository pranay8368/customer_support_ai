ABC Technologies - Pricing Guide

Subscription Plans:

1. Starter Plan - $19/month
   - Up to 5 users
   - Basic reporting
   - Email support

2. Professional Plan - $49/month
   - Up to 20 users
   - Advanced reporting and analytics
   - Priority email and chat support

3. Enterprise Plan - $99/month
   - Unlimited users
   - Custom integrations
   - Dedicated account manager
   - 24/7 phone support

Annual subscriptions receive a 15% discount compared to monthly billing.

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card is required to start the trial.
